Tribute to Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein was a German-born theoretical physicist who developed the theory of relativity, one of the two pillars of modern physics.

His remarkable contributions to the scientific community revolutionized our understanding of the universe, time, and space.

Einstein's most famous equation, E=mc2, demonstrated the equivalence of mass and energy, leading to groundbreaking advancements in nuclear energy.

Apart from his scientific brilliance, Einstein was also an advocate for civil rights, freedom, and peace, using his fame to speak out against discrimination and injustice.
